Ingredients:
 - 3 packages of chopped spinach
 - 1 package of cream cheese
 - 1 stick of butter
 - 1 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs

Directions:
 1. Cook and drain spinach.
 2. Add the cream cheese and half of the butter.  Mix well.
 3. Melt the other half of butter and add breadcrumbs.
 4. Sprinkle on top of spinach.
 5. Bake on 350 for 20 minutes.
